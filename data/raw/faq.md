# Frequently Asked Questions

## What are the working hours?

The parking facility operates daily from 06:00 to 23:00.

## Can I reserve a parking space in advance?

Yes, users can request a parking reservation through the chatbot interface.

## Is the reservation immediately confirmed?

No, reservation requests are reviewed and confirmed by a parking administrator.

## What information is required to make a reservation?

You need to provide:
- First name
- Last name
- Car plate number
- Reservation start and end time

## Are there electric vehicle charging stations?

Yes, EV charging stations are available in designated areas.

## Are there spaces for disabled drivers?

Yes, dedicated parking spaces for disabled drivers are available.

## Is overnight parking allowed?

Overnight parking is allowed only with a confirmed reservation.

## What happens if I exceed my reservation time?

Additional charges may apply based on the parking tariff.

## Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, cancellations are possible before the reservation start time.

## Is the parking area secure?

Yes, the parking area is monitored by CCTV systems.
