# Parking Rules and Regulations

## General Rules

- Vehicles must be parked within marked parking spaces.
- The driver must follow all posted signs and instructions.
- Speed limit inside the parking area is 10 km/h.

## Vehicle Restrictions

- Maximum vehicle height: 2.2 meters
- Oversized vehicles are not allowed
- Only registered vehicles may use reserved spaces

## Safety

- Do not leave valuables inside your vehicle
- Follow instructions of parking staff if present
- Report any suspicious activity to support

## Liability

The parking operator is not responsible for:
- Theft or damage to vehicles
- Loss of personal belongings

## Violations

Failure to comply with the rules may result in:
- Fines
- Removal of the vehicle at owner's expense
- Restriction from using the parking facility
