# Central Parking Wroclaw

Central Parking Wroclaw is a modern public parking facility located near the city center of Wroclaw. It is designed to provide convenient and secure parking for both short-term and long-term visitors.

## General Information

- Total capacity: 120 parking spaces
- Covered parking: Yes
- Open-air parking: Yes
- Electric vehicle charging stations: Available
- Disabled parking spaces: 6
- Maximum vehicle height: 2.2 meters

## Facilities

- 24/7 CCTV monitoring
- Automated entry and exit barriers
- Lighting across all parking areas
- Payment kiosks and online payment options

## Security

The parking area is monitored 24/7 using video surveillance systems. Security personnel may perform routine inspections.
