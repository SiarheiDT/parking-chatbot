# Location and Access

Central Parking Wroclaw is located close to the main city center and is easily accessible by car.

## Address

ul. Swidnicka 45, 50-029 Wroclaw, Poland

## Nearby Landmarks

- Wroclaw Market Square (Rynek) – 5 minutes walking distance
- Wroclaw Opera House – 3 minutes walking distance
- Galeria Dominikanska – 7 minutes walking distance

## Access Instructions

- Entry is available from ul. Swidnicka
- Exit is located on ul. Kazimierza Wielkiego
- Follow road signs marked "Parking"

## Public Transport

- Tram stops within 2 minutes walking distance
- Bus stops within 3 minutes walking distance

## Navigation

The parking location can be easily found using navigation systems by searching for "Central Parking Wroclaw".
